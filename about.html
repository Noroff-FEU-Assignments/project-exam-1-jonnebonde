The 3D-printer

First i want to introduce the machine that are making these objects, the Prusa MK3S with some facts:

  • Build Volume: 250mm*210mm*210mm
  • Filament diameter: 1.75mm
  • Print Surface: Removable magnetic steel sheet
  • Max nozzle temperatur : 300Â°C
  • Supported materials: PLA, PETG, ASA, PC, CPE, PVB, HIPS, PP, Flex, Nylon, Carbon filled, Woodfill.

My 3D-printer are placed inside an server cabinet for temperatur control. For reducing noise and vibrations its placed on a sandwich of noise reduction plate and a 25kg cementblock.
